Upload your Documents

To enable you to study with us, let us help you attach all the correct documents under the right categories! For example: simply select the "Upload your CV" option to attach your CV, "Upload proof of English proficiency" for your English certificate, and so on. Remember, upload only one document per category. In case you have attached a wrong document to a category, then you can delete that file by clicking on the red symbol “X“. Once you have deleted the file, click the selected category one more time and upload the correct document. Before you click the "Upload your documents" button, please check if all your documents have been attached to the right categories. It's that simple!

Documents at a Glance

Upload the documents as listed below using the section above or send them to info-international@iu.org:

  • Curriculum vitae (CV)
  • Undergraduate Degree Certificate

    Please attach an officially certified copy of your degree certificate.

  • Proof of required professional experience [PDF Download]

    Please provide a job reference or an employer's certificate. (Not necessary for Bachelor)

  • Proof of English proficiency

    Please provide proof of English proficiency through TOEFL, IELTS or DUOLINGO, or take our new Speexx programme for free to reach the required English level.

Do you want to contact us via email?

We will take a look at your submitted documents and contact you with any questions we may have. Don’t have all the required documents yet? No problem! Our new Scholarship Programme means you can start learning and send them later!

Start your studies

When we have got everything we require, you can start your programme at IU International University of Applied Sciences. Once you have completed 50% of your course and handed in all necessary documents, you will be automatically registered as a student.
